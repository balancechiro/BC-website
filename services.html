Treatment

Thorough physicals and evaluations

 

Dr. Yang will take a full health history by obtaining information on your symptoms, lifestyle, your family's health history and any previous injuries and treatments. A physical examination will then be performed which will include orthopedic and neurological testing to help reveal the condition of your spine and areas of musculoskeletal tenderness, muscle spasm, joint mobility and range of motion. Based on the findings and your condition, the doctor can individualize a treatment plan for you and answer any questions you may have.

 

The most updated chiropractic care

 

Dr. Yang utilizes the most appropriate chiropractic techniques for each individual. By attending continuing education seminars, she maintains updated on the latest research about chiropractic treatments and their effectiveness.

 

Physiotherapy and rehabilitation

 

Physiotherapy helps aid the healing process to rehabilitate the spine and other parts of the body. Our office uses modalities such as heat therapy, cold therapy, intersegmental traction, electrical stimulation, and joint mobilization, along with therapeutic exercise, which includes stretching, strengthening, and postural modification to rehabilitate the spine and prevent re-injury. Our goal is to help the patient return to function as quickly as possible. This is attempted by reducing pain, increasing flexibility, restoring range of motion and function, building strength, and correcting posture.

 

Spinal and postural screenings

 

Posture and spinal screenings can reveal important health information and unlock the door to improved health and well-being. We provide screenings at community events, health fairs and places of employment. Please contact our office and we will be happy to arrange a screening at your event or workplace.

 

Trigger point therapy/Myofascial release

 

Trigger points are uncontrolled muscle spasms, which cause referred pain to other areas of the body. These muscle spasms cause pain, decrease range of motion, and increase muscle weakness. Trigger point therapy is the act of decreasing these muscle spasms. The spasms are relieved by sustaining pressure over a short period of time. This type of therapy helps reduce chronic muscle spasms, avoid scar tissue formation, improve muscle tone, and increase range of motion.
