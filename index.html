Balance Chiropractic - For a well-adjusted life!

 

At Balance Chiropractic, we are committed to relieving our patients' body pain and improving their overall health and well-being using the principles of chiropractic care.

 

We are results-oriented professionals who provide a step-by-step treatment program to help our patients achieve their goals, whether they are to alleviate pain, optimize mobility and comfort, improve posture, speed up recovery time or increase performance in their daily lives.

 

Our patients are more effective, confident, independent, and have more energy than before coming to us. We help them enjoy productive lives instead of nursing injuries and being in pain. Now many of them spend more time doing the things they enjoy and experience a greater level of fulfillment. Read our patient Success Stories!

 

We are located in the heart of Old Town Monrovia, CA just 8 miles east of Pasadena, conveniently located near Arcadia, Duarte, and Sierra Madre. Balance Chiropractic patients enjoy professional quality care in a new facility.

 

We accept most health insurance, including PPO, POS, EPO and HMO plans.
